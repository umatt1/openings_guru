About this form:

Markov chains are a model of predicting future events based off of previous events fed into the model. This webapp uses the twitter API to grab tweets from a provided twitter handle to generate similar tweets. The order of the model refers to how many words to look at when deciding probability (i.e. an order of 1 looks at what probabilities of words after individual words, order of 2 looks at words that come after combinations of 2 words). Additionally, if the ID you provide doesn't match valid twitter handle, it grabs from my twitter account (@BurkhardMatt)
